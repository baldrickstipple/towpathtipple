About

Baldrick's Towpath Tipple is a floating canal bar serving boaters, towpath walkers and canal lovers across the UK waterways.

Our mission is simple: celebrate canal culture, serve great drinks, and create a welcoming waterside meeting place wherever the boats moor.

The Boats

Baldrick

Baldrick is the motor boat powering the operation, towing Tipple around the canal network to festivals, towpath gatherings and boating events.

Tipple

Tipple (formerly the butty TAM) is being transformed into a dedicated floating bar with solar power, upgraded electrics and a proper beer dispense system.
